ECC SAMPLE BANKING RFP FIXTURE
State Bank of Pakistan — Core Banking System Modernisation
Document Reference: SBP-RFP-2024-CBM-001
Version: 1.0 (Test Fixture — 10 explicit requirements)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 3 — FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 The system must provide a real-time account balance inquiry
    facility for all account types (current, savings, fixed deposit)
    accessible via both web portal and mobile application.

3.2 The system must support multi-currency transactions including
    PKR, USD, EUR, and GBP with automatic exchange rate conversion
    sourced from the SBP inter-bank rate feed.

3.3 The system must generate automated daily reconciliation reports
    for all branch accounts and submit them to the head office
    treasury module by 23:59 PKT each business day.

3.4 The system must allow relationship managers to initiate and
    track loan applications end-to-end, from document collection
    through credit scoring, approval workflow, and disbursement.

3.5 The system must integrate with the RAAST instant payment
    infrastructure to process inbound and outbound transfers
    within 10 seconds of instruction receipt.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 4 — NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 The system must encrypt all customer data at rest using
    AES-256 encryption and all customer PII in transit using
    TLS 1.3 or higher — in full compliance with SBP-2024
    data protection directives and ISO-27001 controls.

4.2 The system must maintain 99.95% uptime availability measured
    on a rolling 30-day window, excluding pre-approved maintenance
    windows not exceeding 4 hours per month.

4.3 The system must support a minimum of 5,000 concurrent users
    with transaction response times not exceeding 2 seconds at
    95th percentile under peak load conditions.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 5 — COMPLIANCE AND SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 The system must maintain a tamper-evident audit log of all
    user actions, administrative changes, and transaction events.
    Audit logs must be retained for a minimum of 7 years per
    SBP-2024 record retention requirements. All access to audit
    logs must require MFA authentication.

5.2 The procurement of this system is subject to PPRA-2024
    regulations. The vendor must submit a single-source justification
    or participate in open tender evaluation. The evaluation committee
    will score submissions on technical merit (60%) and commercial
    terms (40%).
